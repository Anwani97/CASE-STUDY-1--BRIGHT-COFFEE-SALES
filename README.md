Bright Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

📌 Overview

This project analyzes sales data from a coffee shop to understand revenue performance, customer purchasing behavior, product trends, and store-level performance. The analysis focuses on identifying peak sales periods, best-selling products, and opportunities to improve underperforming areas.

🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to uncover key business insights from sales data and provide actionable recommendations to improve revenue, optimize product offerings, and enhance customer engagement across different store locations.

🛠️ Tools Used
SQL
Excel
Power BI
Miro

🔍 Methodology
Data Cleaning
Prepared and structured raw sales data for analysis
Data Analysis
Analyzed revenue by product, category, and store location
Evaluated customer purchasing patterns by time of day
Visualization
Created dashboards and charts to highlight trends and comparisons
Insight Generation
Identified high-performing products, peak sales times, and underperforming areas

📈 Key Insights
Brewed beverages (Brewed Black Tea, Brewed Chai Tea, Barista Espresso) generate the highest revenue
Peak sales occur during rush hour and mid-morning, while late evenings show low performance
Tea and coffee categories dominate sales volume, with bakery items performing well for upselling opportunities
Lower Manhattan store outperforms other locations, while Astoria and Hell’s Kitchen show growth potential

💡 Recommendations
Improve underperforming products
Bundle low-performing items with best-sellers
Introduce seasonal promotions
Focus marketing on low-sales periods
Launch happy hour deals and late-afternoon combos
Implement loyalty rewards during non-peak hours
Increase stock for high-demand items
Ensure availability of top sellers like Barista Espresso and Brewed Teas
Promote high-margin specialty drinks
